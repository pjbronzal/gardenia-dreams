Our Story

Gardenia Dreams was created by Ann Marie and Precious as a labor of love. Born during the Kodego MP2 project, our passion for nature and beauty led us to this journey.

We're on a mission to bring the beauty of nature into your life through exquisite flowers, thoughtful gifts, and unforgettable moments. Every bouquet, plant, and keepsake at Gardenia Dreams tells a story and creates lasting memories.

Join us on this floral adventure, and let Gardenia Dreams be a part of your story.
